Here is a world I played.
http://server.carbontechsoftware.com/Worlds/hhgb_9766.cwf


It's the biggest custom world made on Carbon Combat. it has lots and lots of trees (very laggy). I loaded the world when the inventory wasn't finished loading so I had defaults. Only thing i had was my weapons. I found out that the map is glitched. THis happens everytime.




I exited my car and the car wouldn't blow up. It has been about 30 seconds. As you can see my car has racer wheels. The newbies' and guests' cars look like this.


You can also see that my car's gun is pointing at me lol

Update:
I entered hover mode (press and hold space, press enter twice, release space) then exited my vehicle. I found out I could control the vehicle when it is in hover mode even if I am in soldier! I pressed my arrow keys to move soldier and it moved the car! Unfortunately, space key didn't control the car so I couldn't activate hover mode.
