Oh btw there's a way to change the CWF data to a link.
  • open your text editor and paste in the data
  • save it with extension ".cwf". If you cant do it, save as ".txt" and cahnge the extension to ".cwf"
  • put it in your server or Dropbox and get the link
U now got a link!
although I recommend just loading it in the map maker and getting a link.
