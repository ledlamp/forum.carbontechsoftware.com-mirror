Feedback & Ideas / A couple of ideas
« on: Late August, 2012 »
Well hi everyone!

Today I have finally been able to play Carbon Combat in a very long time! My old computer refused to run it, but I have now had an upgrade, and I can actually play!!

So, this feature request is very simple really... I'd like to be able to switch to my other weapon if one of my weapons is reloading. As I a laser cannon most of the time (finally got the credits to buy it back after being away so long), it's kinda annoying to have to wait ages for my laser to reload before I can fire anything again (trigger finger itching...). So for a n00b like me, the ability to use a second weapon while my first weapon is reloading would be very convenient.

Oh, I also have a smaller feature request as well (just didn't want to spam the forum with two posts at five-minute-intervals). I think players should be able to see how much time a round has got left in game selection menu. This would mean that players could also pick their game depending on how much time it has left.

Other than that, great game! CC has improved a lot since I last played it, good job, CarbonTech!

What are your thoughts on this?
