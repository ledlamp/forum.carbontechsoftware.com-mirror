In this thread you can share your whiteboard drawings instead of making a new topic.

What is the whiteboard?
It is a multiplayer/multiuser drawing tool that you can use to draw stuff in real-time with others.


Where can I access the whiteboard?
The whitebaord used to be accessed by a button in the chat, but it seems that the button has been removed. You can access the board at this link:
http://flockdraw.com/CarbonCombat


How do I save a drawing?
Just click the save button in the bar and copy the link!



How do I embed the drawing in the forum?
  • Click the save button.
  • Copy the link and open it.
  • Right-click it and click "Copy image URL"
  • Click the insert image button in the toolbar on the forum and enter the URL.
Example images:
http://flockdraw.com/gallery/view/1616078
