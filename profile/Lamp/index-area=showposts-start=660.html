Feedback & Ideas / Embed Videos
« on: Late August, 2012 »
I saw on another forum where the owner added a special BBcode that can embed YouTube videos. I think BBcode is customizable, so CarbonTech can make it. It could be like this:


Code: [Select]
[embed]http://youtu.be/nX1YzS_CYIw[/embed]

Or it could be in a box with a title like this:


Code: [Select]
[embed=Kittens and kittycats meowing]http://youtu.be/nX1YzS_CYIw[/embed]

The title would go after the equal sign and before the right bracket.




Here is another example:

Code: [Select]
[embed=(title)](link to youtube video)[/embed]

Replace "(title)" with the title. Replace "(link to youtube video)" with the link to the youtube video.


Links should be able to be used with shortened URLs.
Examples:
http://youtu.be/nX1YzS_CYIw (youtube shortened link)
http://tinyurl.com/kittens-cats-meow (TinyURL link)
http://bit.ly/RoWoDV (bitmark)



There should also be a button in the toolbar for it. Click the button, an alert appears with a box to put the link, paste it in, click OK, and the video will appear.
