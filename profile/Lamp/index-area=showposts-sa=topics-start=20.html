How to upload an image for newbies


Step 1: First you will need to host it on the internet. I am going to use imgur for this tutorial.

Step 2: Now you need to insert the image into your post.
Copy the BBcode or Linked BBcode.



What is the difference between BBcode and Linked BBcode?
BBcode will show only the image. Linked BBcode will show the image and have it hyperlinked, meaning people can click it to go to the image on imgur.


That's it! You have inserted an image into your post!


If you don't want to put the image in the post, you can attach your image.

« on: Mid-November, 2012 »

Why it's great to be level 1 in Carbon Combat (only if you are skilled in real life; know how to play well):
  • You get a lot of credits and XP when you kill higher level players!
  • You get to play with the basics and work your way up
  • You don't lose much when you are killed by a higher level player
  • You can act like a n00b
  • You play with rocket launcher and plasma blaster (fun!)
  • You don't care about rank!
  • You get to explore all the weapons!
  • You get to fight car bots and not be bored!
  • Bots are easier on you (i think).
Why it sux to be a newbie (in real life; new to the game):
  • Pros kill you and you get mad.
