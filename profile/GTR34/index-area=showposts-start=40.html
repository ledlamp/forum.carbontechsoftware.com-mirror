The Devastator





Muscle car concept by GTR34
Amongst all the supercar influence and Bugatti - like styling in CC, I think it's time that a fresh new car idea should be presented. Behold the Devastator - an intimidating muscle car designed with brute force and sheer destructive capability. Featuring a large 40mm cannon (demo weapon) and 4.8l V8 engine, this car is a must have for all the true muscle car fans.
In order to keep the balance, this car has brutal acceleration and top speed, but sluggish handling characteristics and rather high weight - as expected from a muscle car. Regular weapons can be equipped and it has all the capabilities of the standard car. Armor (hopefully) coming soon.
This took me about 2 days to make. I haven't been on CC in a while and thought I should once again contribute something that could spice up the gameplay. Comment a "Yes" if you want to see this car in - game!
