« on: Late November, 2012 »
The majority of us have submitted several ideas in the recent past, and nearly all have been debated. So what I want to do is a community exersize here. This is a competition to see who can get the most agreed upon idea submitted, and the most often. If you want to win the competition, you may have to scrap an idea you think is good for an idea you think other people think is good.
 
TEH ALMIGHTY RULES:
  • The idea is limited to a piece of weapons or equipment, so no one submits a no-brainer idea that everyone wants (IE option to hide bots from server list)
  • The idea can be similar to a current weapon, but it has to be new, original, and not talked about on the forum before.
  • Once every regular forum member has seen this contest (you will have to make a post here to say if you want to not join, or submit an idea to join), the contest will end and voting will begin.
  • Once each person in the contest has voted on their favorite idea and we have eliminated any ties for last place, we eliminate the least favorite idea and do another round of voting, so if your favorite idea is eliminated, you can still compromise and vote for another idea.
  • You can vote on your own idea (since the poll is anonymous and it would be impossible to tell if you who voted on who's idea), and if you didn't submit an idea you can still vote.
  • We go until only one idea is left. The winner gets bragging rights.
I would join the contest, so that gives at least one entrant. We need at least two, so if someone submits an idea the contest is on!

For the record, this is my way of trying to help the community repair itself and get at least a bit more agreeable and accepting, including me.
