The reason I think this would be OP is for one of the same reasons the shotgun is OP: the spread actually helps at medium to long distances. You don't need a perfect shot, you just need to aim in the general direction to score some damage.


To help explain, think of the minigun: at medium to long range you don't score any hits simply because the bullets have no spread, forcing you to place the cursor leading the target which isn't an exact science and so results in misses, even if you are skilled with the minigun. With spread, you aim near the target and bullets fly in an area, which would include the area that you would need to aim to score a hit when leading a target.
