Feedback & Ideas / Re: Ninja invisibility armour
« on: Mid-March, 2013 »
Invisibility armour should:
  • Have a decreased alpha so it's is harder to see - at the moment we can still very much see (in)visible cars.
  • No mini-map icon flicker, since you have to stay under 20kmh.
  • Stay invisible indefinitely, there should be no timer for invisible armour people.
  • The alpha should increase steadily between 20kmh and 30kmh, so you gain visibility gradually.
  • Prevent the ability to pickup the invisibility pickup.
Invisibility pickup should:
  • Have a higher alpha so it's easier to see.
  • Mini-map icon flicker.
  • Have a timer.
  • Invisibility regardless of speed.
One other thing that would be real nice a gear change system, with the clutch being bound to button G or something - with the first gear taking you up to 15-20kmh, making it easier to:
  • Cruise at a max speed that isn't the car's top speed.
  • Cruise under 20kmh to keep the invisibility while not having to tap the accelerate button so you don't accidentally go over 20kmh because you tapped at the wrong time.

Feedback & Ideas / Re: New Game Design/Animations
« on: Mid-February, 2013 »
Graphics
1. I think explosions look a little like 2D sprite-y at the moment and it would be nice if Carbon Tech partnered with a 3D animator / artist to make a good looking explosion - the fire resides too long and looks cartoonish.
2. Multiple light sources can be intensive on low end computers - the headlights and lights from explosions ideas must be toggle-able for low end computers.
3. I think grass sprites that wave in the breeze should be used on the high end of settings because it can smooth out the sharp edges that Unity ground textures have.
4. Dents are the kind of thing that will either look great but be intensive or look like crap and run smoothly. Sorry.
5. Reloading animations - yes please! This is a great idea but it would have to be done well or don't do it at all.
