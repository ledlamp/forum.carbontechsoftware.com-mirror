Carbon Combat / How to get premium credits
« on: Late June, 2013 »
If anyone asks you how to get premium creds, all you have to do is link them here, no more endlessly explaining how.

1. (Note that this is done on the official site, play.carbontechsoftware.com)
Click on the silver coin after you have logged in. Make sure you are not in fullscreen.

2.
Select premium creds.

3.
If you wish to pay for them with real money, fill in your username in the boxes and continue.
For free earn offers, scroll down and select an offer of your choice, note that some offers may be phishing scams, viruses or other malicous content. Use common sense and anti-virus software to avoid these. Also note that though you may have done everything correctly they might not work. This service is not provided by the dev and is not affiliated with him. Any complaints should be directed to them on their website


4.
If you choose to use paypal then this screen will show up, paypal will guide you through the process. Upon completion they should be processed very quickly, around 5 mins.


If you have chosen to do an offer then if it works they should be given within 10-20 mins.

BUG (READ!)

If there are any creds left over then buy some other items until you can't buy anything else even if you don't particularly want them, before exiting.


The game currently does not support buying premium items with Kong Kreds.

Feedback & Ideas / Teleport item
« on: Late April, 2013 »
This item teleports you to an upgradable distance of 100-200m (undecided)(100mtrs is around 3 and a bit bases) away from your current position, the upgrade could add an extra 5mtrs per upgrade.
It takes 2 secounds to charge, your car remains frozen for that duration, the last 1/2 secound you are invinsible.

The default direction (not pressing any keys) teleports you forwards, but you can teleport 9 ways altogether, up by pressing space, wasd moves you the respective directions, you can combine two of the wasd keys to teleport diagonally.
The 2 secounds it takes to charge gives you time to press the right key to go in the direction you want, or if you change your mind, time to switch keys as the direction is finalised after the 2 secs are up.

The animation for it could be a blue glowing outline that turns black then fades once you have teleported.

The cooldown is 30 secounds, I'm not sure whether there should be a limit, I don't really think so as the max you could do it in a standard 10 min game is 18-19 times.

This is great for both attack and defense as it allows for escape and surprise attacks, or pursuing someone (maybe even overtaking them completely and appearing in front of them like a ghost.)
Also, unless you think it should be disabled whilst you have the flag, escaping the enemy base in CTF, even if it is you can still use it to sneak in.

Edit: maybe you can teleport down as well, with ctrl, if there's a way to stop you ending up below the terrain.
