Carbon Combat / How to get premium credits
« on: Late June, 2013 »
If anyone asks you how to get premium creds, all you have to do is link them here, no more endlessly explaining how.

1. (Note that this is done on the official site, play.carbontechsoftware.com)
Click on the silver coin after you have logged in. Make sure you are not in fullscreen.

2.
Select premium creds.

3.
If you wish to pay for them with real money, fill in your username in the boxes and continue.
For free earn offers, scroll down and select an offer of your choice, note that some offers may be phishing scams, viruses or other malicous content. Use common sense and anti-virus software to avoid these. Also note that though you may have done everything correctly they might not work. This service is not provided by the dev and is not affiliated with him. Any complaints should be directed to them on their website


4.
If you choose to use paypal then this screen will show up, paypal will guide you through the process. Upon completion they should be processed very quickly, around 5 mins.


If you have chosen to do an offer then if it works they should be given within 10-20 mins.

BUG (READ!)
There is a bug, that after buying something with premium creds, the next time you log in all your creds will dissapear. Buy all the items you wish. If there are any creds left over then buy some other items until you can't buy anything else even if you don't particularly want them, before exiting.


The game currently does not support buying premium items with Kong Kreds.
