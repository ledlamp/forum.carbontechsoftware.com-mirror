So I have this idea for a new car in the game... Well it's not really a car, but a truck... A really, big, truck.

I haven't really thought of an image in my head yet, but I picture it being either a semi truck, or a monster truck, with the front of the truck looking like some sort of evil monster (Hence the name). It could have glowing red headlights (eyes), the grill of the truck could look like sharp teeth (mouth), and it could have a massive engine coming out of the hood (just for looks)... If you play the newest Twisted Metal on PS3, Picture Hammerhead or Slayer.

It would have a VERY HIGH top speed (175 - 200 MPH. If you're wondering why, it has very slow acceleration, so it should be worth it to be able to eventually go almost as fast as the spectator orb, it just makes sense to me.), very, very slow acceleration (0 to top speed in about 90 seconds), decent handling (about the same as the Devastator), and almost no flight agility (It's not meant to fly, it's a monster/semi truck, it's supposed to stay on the ground). It would also have A LOT of health (like having level 20 heavy armor), and ramming an enemy at high speeds would take out about 1/3 of their health (if they have no armor).

It would be insanely expensive though, this baby comes expensive or doesn't come at all: You CAN NOT RENT IT, you can only purchase it permanently for 100,000 credits.

Note: The vehicle wouldn't be overly massive, just bigger than the other cars. It would still be able to fit in all the regular maps. For example: The bunkers on Battle Island, considering you spawn in them...

I know this probably won't be added to the game, especially since the developer is gone, but I just REALLY wanted to share this idea...
