Carbon Combat / Re: this isnt really cool with me
« on: Late July, 2012 »
Percents show your progress to the next level (i.e. Elite, Newbie, Rookie). Each level except for the 1st to 2nd are spaced 1000 XP apart. Thus, 10 XP = 1%; if you only earn 3 XP your level percent will not change. Also, keep in mind that the percent is floored, so if you have 99% you could have anywhere from 990 to 999 XP for that particular level. The best way to check to see if you got your XP is to go directly into your profile tab and see exactly how many experience points you have.

It has a much lower poly count than CarbonTech's model, so you may prefer using this. Also, it's in OBJ format, which can be imported directly into Sketchup, whilst FBX can't.

Download it here: http://dl.dropbox.com/u/41103673/Corkscrew.obj.zip

You'll want to put some loop slices or else what you'll end up with is a very bumpy corkscrew (the quads will be split into tris on import into Unity which will make the outer edge uneven).
