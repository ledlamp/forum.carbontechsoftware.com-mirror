Feedback & Ideas / Animation(s) [Please read fully before commenting]
« on: Mid-February, 2013 »
So we all know that what we can do as the Soldier is limited (for now...) and some of us would like to have more options. So I've made a list of Animations (or Actions) that the Soldier should be able to do:
#1: Vaulting: In the discipline of parkour, a vault is any type of movement that involves jumping or leaping over an obstacle while putting weight on it with one's hands. Parkour vaults enable the practitioner (traceur) to quickly and efficiently pass over railings, tables, walls, and platforms.

I'm some many of you have done this in real life for various reasons (At least I did... >_<), and know how it works. You vault over (or on top of) an object in your way.
#2 Crawling: The leopard crawl is a military-specific crawl. There are two versions, the leopard crawl proper and a modified version for when carrying weapons in the hands. This is a two-beat gait like a trot: an arm/elbow is advanced with the diagonal knee. This is designed for the smallest silhouette possible, and the body is often nearly or actually touching the ground, and although the elbow and knee are the main focus, most of the respective limbs touch the ground.

#3 Cover: A cover system is video game gameplay mechanic that allows a virtual avatar to avoid dangers, usually in a three-dimensional (and occasionally two-dimensional) world. This method is a digital adaptation of the real-life military tactic of taking cover to dodge enemy gunfire or explosives.

#4 Melee Combat: "Melee" has been adopted and popularized as a term in war-gaming, board games, tabletop role-playing games and video games to encompass all close-quarter fighting and directly striking an opponent at ranges generally less than a few feet with, but not only, fists, feet, knives, rifle-butts or any other melee weapon or special spells or tactics with the intention of causing harm.

(But with a gun... Ya'll know what I mean...)
#5 Roll: An evasive action or evasive maneuver is an exercise aimed at avoiding an object, attack, or opponent.

#6 Ledge Traversal: The Ledge Traversal was a move could be perform be preformed in some game like Ratchet & Clank and Splinter Cell. First they had to jump on to a ledge, doing a Ledge Grab, then move to the left or right, traversing the ledge. The Ledge Traversal along with the Ledge Grab was useful for navigating through difficult platforms, and small areas. To do a Ledge Traversal you had to navigate to the left or right by using the Left Analog stick.

Thanks for reading, comments, suggestions and constructive critisim is welcomed.

Feedback & Ideas / Cops and Robbers :D
« on: Mid-November, 2012 »
So...
I was online (About 2 minutes ago), checking up on a few interesting threads I missed.
The first two I looked at were:
http://forum.carbontechsoftware.com/feedback-ideas/police-body-kit (Police Bodykit Thread)
http://forum.carbontechsoftware.com/feedback-ideas/smoke-grenade (Smoke Grenade Thread)
I think so seen where I'm going with this...
The proposed game mode (Called Cops and Robbers) would work this way:
 
Team A (Cops) would try to capture Team B (Robbers). Team A would play the game this way: Take out all 4 tires of a member of Team B (Each Tire with a health of 25 each. Then (Assuming they have the Inflatable Metal Cage), they press "2" , and they back up the cage until the Team B Member (Called "Bob" for now), is loaded into the cage. Then, they must succesfully transport the prisoner to Team A's base, without Team B breaking out the prisoner (Which can happen by: A, the cage is destroyed, or B, The "Cop" is killed). Then, when the prisoner is taken to the base (Successfully of course), the "Cop" gets 150 credits for the capture (35 for a kill, 21 for assist), plus 1 point for the team.
 
Team B would play the game this way: They would try to steal the "impound safe", which normally holds 2500 credits (Shared with the whole team), or the most recently impounded weapons (The weapons would be the same as the ones impounded from the most recently captured Team B Member), which would last the whole round. Team B would have to successfully transport the Impound Safe to they're base, without being killed or captured. Once the safe is taken to they're base, they receive a share of the money and 50 extra credits (Plus 1 point for Team B).
(I need a minute to figure out how to get this to work for Soldier...)
 
Neccessary features:

Neccessary models:

Robber Bodykit

Impound Safe
