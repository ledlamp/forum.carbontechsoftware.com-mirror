Off Topic / Carbon Combat's Great Depression/Reflection
« on: Late May, 2013 »
I look at what is going on with CC, and I notice that the community and the game has reached a sudden outburst of negativity and problems. The complaints of n00bs using OP weapons are talked about every other day, people lose their accounts to strangers and we are not growing or benefiting from the help of our Admin.

I reflect a lot on what can be done to help better others' ideas, problems, and concerns. We don't see mods as much as we used to, CT has not been seen for a while, and many of us have come to wonder when he will come back for sure, if at all.

Now, you say things like, "It is just an academic problem." However, you look back and think about how it was just before CT had left. It wasn't much better. Our first real change that we found out to be exciting and different was the different ways was the new flying system. Soon after, we gained the rental system, that many did not agree with at first. Bodykits became exclusive to Prem. Credits. Levels become harder to fully achieve level 20 items and weapons. CC changed, and for the better.

Our game seemed to be hitting a huge curve when the shotgun came around. We never realized how good we had it until the shotgun showed its true colors. Now, the weapon has destroyed the feeling that CC once gave out to others. The feeling of fun, strategy, and excitement. Now it's a game of cat n' mouse between shotguns and Health Pick-ups. If that didn't bring us down lower, the Railgun simple did the trick. Though we have had it for a while, it has started to become a bother to many of the players. With about 80% of the power of a laser, this gun starting ravaging the very thought of "escape." There was nothing anyone could really do as a player other than run. Run from things that do not need to be chased by. Through the insecurity of others, the way of the n00b has spread though the worlds and maps of Carbon Combat. These problems have such a massive impact on the reality of the game. On what happens now, and what others want to happen.

Even though we have these problems, it is not anything that can't be fixed. We, the Community, are a part of something that we take for granted. We don't realize that as a whole, we have fun, we fight, we talk, and we play; Just like a family. Even though we may not have the perfect Carbon Combat game we want, we can still work together, as a family, to make it that way. Families fight, laugh, and enjoy the very fact that they have each other. We can do the same, and better the sake of CC through the help of each other.

Keep Playing guys; It only gets better.

~UDICTED2MARVEL

Feedback & Ideas / New Shotgun Suggestion (Please Comment)
« on: Mid-April, 2013 »
Hello Carbon Combat Community!

For many months now, the shotgun has become a major problem for our users as many users see it as "the gun that killed CC."

However, I have come up with an idea, that may or may not have come up in the past, to solve the whole idea of the shotgun killing the fun of the game.

This simple idea is a "Slug." Currently, ten rounds are fired at a time; each bullet deals from 4-8 hit points. 10bulletsx8dmg=80HP. Two rounds to an unarmored car and they have no chance.

Half of the time, people don't even aim the slightest bit to hit their target. they point and shoot. But if the ten rounds we use currently was switched over to a slug, the whole concept of dying in two shot would disappear.

Now, I am not saying we switch the rounds to a slug and keep the damage at 4-8 HP per round. I am saying change the rounds to a slug and have the slug deal a great amount of damage then one bullet currently. I would say about 20 damage for each slug. 20dmgx5bullets=100HP; a reasonable amount that could be dealt with a shotgun. I would say 25, but that require the shooter only 4 rounds, with still, can be a devastating amount of damage.

I believe this idea will, not not solve, reduce our shotgun problem many users have. This weapon may be a hated one currently for some, but it doesn't have to be.

Please explain your thoughts below on what you believe should happen.
