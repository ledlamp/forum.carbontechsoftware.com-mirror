Now, I'm going to say it: most of these suggestions are almost completely cosmetic. Why are they worth suggesting? Simply put, they give an appearance of something, most of which is "that was cool". Let me start:
 
  • Screen shake: Simple, anything that would shake the car (or would in real life) shakes the camera. It adds a bit of stun value and intensity to collisions and explosives. The camera shake would however never exceed a certain point so the player's screen is never obstructed.
  • Dive cone: When moving downwards past a certain (high) speed a red cone of flames appears below it, like during space re-entry. Adds a bit of sci-fi and action to the game, and could simply be a slightly transparent cone with a red, possibly animated texture. Sorry I couldn't get a picture, surprisingly hard.
  • Drift: Yes, drifting. An explanation for the near perfect turning, and could simply be the car tilting into the turn or a full gameplay feature with smokey tires and the works. Think I'll make that a poll.
  • Gun sounds: Basically louder sounds for firing and delivering a weapon to a target. For instance, when firing a rocket a small click to signal the fuel being "lit", a whoosh sound during flight and a satisfying BANG for the explosion. The perception of damage goes well with the perception of taking damage with screen shake too. Explosive canisters could get louder as well.
Discuss.
