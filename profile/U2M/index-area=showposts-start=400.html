Hello Fellas!
 
To start off, I don't remember if this was mentioned (the name) but the weapon itself is a whole new kind of weapon. One that is in the game yet.
 
Let me explain:
     It's called an Energy Cannon. This cannon Will bring a new type of weapon to the game through area affect. No, not like explosive, but like a lasting area affect. When the round is fired, It travels just as fast as a fully charged grenade, just without the arch the grenade give. It will travel at this speed forever until it hits an object. When it hits a target or surface, a bright, blue ball of energy expands out in the area hit. The energy sphere would only expand out to about the size of the area damage for a grenade now. It would last forever, but it would last.
 
      Stats:
          -Area Effect: Lasts for about 5-10 seconds, depending on level. About as big as area damage of a grenade.
          -Damage: If hit directly by the Energy Cannon, 85% of your life (unarmored car). The area effects are only about 12-15% every second a target or object is in contact. As the area effect start to deteriorate, the damage is lowered.
          -Cost: Will be decided by the community.
 
If you like this idea for the new weapon comment or PM me. I will let CT know if enough people see this weapon as a good addition.
 
Thanks!
