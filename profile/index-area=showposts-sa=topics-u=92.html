Feedback & Ideas / Limits on who can join a game.
« on: Early October, 2012 »
Recently there's been a lot of backlash against Shotgun users and overall "pros killing newbies" situations. So I thought, in other multiplayer games there are features to limit who can join certain games; why not apply this to Carbon Combat, to save much grief?

There could be limits based on:

Equipment: Only allowing people who have certain guns or cars to join game.


Experience: Only allowing people with a set XP to join the game, separating the Elite from the Newbies.


Personally I like the second limitation the most. However, since a level 1 newbie can buy a fully upgraded Shotgun, my entire post may have just been put to rest. But it certainly can't hurt to consider the prospects of such a feature. What do you think.
