The Android Project (Revisited)
posted Late December, 2012
Hello all,


As you may be aware, in the past I presented an idea known as the Android Project. The main idea behind the android project is to develop weapons, armor, and (maybe) bodykits/skins for the android character in Carbon Combat. The hope is that the community will help contribute ideas and help create the project, rather than having the entire thing being done by only one person. My personal progress on the Android Project can be found here, at http://ccandroidproject.blogspot.ca/. I will try to keep it updated regularly, not only with personal progress, but also with the progress of others in helping develop new store items for the Android in Carbon Combat.


Suggestions, comments, and ideas are welcome! Feel free to contribute.


Sincerely,
