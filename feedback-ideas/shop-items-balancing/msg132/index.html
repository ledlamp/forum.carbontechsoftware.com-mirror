Shop Items Balancing
posted Mid-July, 2012
Often new ideas proposed (as new weapons, new items, new armor for CC) end up in a wider discussion on how to balance them with existing goods from the shop; There comes a dielmma, on how to balance the new propositions to a system, which is itself unbalanced - in its current state.

  So here is the good old post about balancing shop items.
 To The Players Who Feel Like Posting Here:
  -this is not a thread to add new suggestions for new items -this is not a thread to discuss about the balnce between humanoids/car/helicopeters and any other engine that might be added in the future

The aim of this discussion is to provide CT with feedback from the players' side on how to balance what is ALREADY IS IN STORE - body kits,weapons,armors, mines and items.   So -stating the obvious- this is a discussion for players that have tried various setups for their cars, (various bodykits, armors, weapons etc) and felt like some items were useless or had been recently nerfed too much, while some were so overpowered that became the "obvious -only- choice".   Please refrain from requesting to have reduced in power a specific weapon/armor only because you have met a player with that weapon/armor, who can own you easilly. Most likely there is some difference in skill involved too. FIRST try that weapon/armor yourself and learn how to use it, then if it still feels OP, please post here. Bare in mind - it usually becomes evident if a request is made out of personal interest/preference.


To CT, Administration, Mods, Developers etc:    The aim of this thread isn't to provide you with extra work (as implementing new items would be), but actually to help you tweak the range of unbalance between the items that have already been implemented, in order to render the gaming experience more diverse, by giving more freedom of choice to CC players amongst a variety of evenly matched options in the shop.

PS
This could also become a great place for CT to communicate to the community when changes efectively are made, so we(Players) won't be left wandering....

EVERYTHING is in relation to the rest.
each weapon should have a specific ideal range and "reign", or almost so, if used correctly.
each armour should have a specific use and "reign", or almost so, if used correctly.
WEAPONS:
ROCKET LAUNCHER
i used to think this was the best balanced weapon, before the introduction of the shotgun.
My opinion is, that  should be as good as the shotgun, or better, on point blank shooting.
I woudl suggest keeping the lvl 1 weapon damage as it is, while rising the lvl 20 damage on an unarmored car to approx 50%, respective to the current 39%.
This weapon is also absorbed BIG TIME by the heavy armour, while the ninja regen doesn't cope with it well. this said, the ninja regen is an armour for long distance battling,so the 2 should hardly ever come in contact.
PLASMA BLASTER
i would suggest increasing the lvl 20 weapon damage of about 4 or 5%, to make up for the difference both with the rocket launcher but also with the shotgun

GRENADE LAUNCHER and EMP GRENADE LAUNCHER
this fellows deal a lot of damage, but are unusable due to the curve of the projectile.
moreover, they are COMPLETELY uselss vs flying opponents(so vs any decent player).
I woudl suggest drasticaly increasing the radiuses(maybe with the upgrades to lvl 20?)
-of the explosion of the grenade( so how wide does it deal damage, not how much)
-of the EMP wave of the EMP grenade(so not how much damage it deals, nor how wide it does so, but how wide it stuns enemies and friends alike).
I'd like to see the range of these two go up to like 10 meters or more, for them to become an integrating part of the gamplay

MISSILE LAUNCHER
potentially the slowest killer of all. In practice it looses in direct fights vs railgun, and laser, while being a decent complementary weapon to both of them, and to the newborn shotgun.
my suggestion would be, to lower it's impact damage(not the explosion one) of a TINY bit more, and bring it up to the same firing speed, number of bullets and reload time of the rocket launcher. That way it woudl become a tactical weapon for flyers.

FLAMETHROWER
in my opinion is a balanced weapon for a very specific use.could deal a tiny more damage, in case the grenades' radiuses would be increased.

RAILGUN
used to feel OP. now i'm more thinking along the lines of it being the only balanced weapon i use; it feels UP towards the shotgun(as the shotgun has a MUCH wider range of application)

LASER CANNON
if it weren't glitching sometimes, i'd say it's balanced. I'd maybe suggest to shorten the charging to fire time, of about half a second, to make it ever so slightly more usable.

SHOTGUN
pls, check out the figures: it dominates over any close range weapon(including the specialised ones, like grenades, rocket launchers AND flamethrowers), mid range it dominates rightfully (alltho the plasma blaster shoudl pose at least a TAD more of a challenge), it is dangerous also on mid long distance due to the  damage of each single fragment of a bullet. Fighting  the shotgun in mid range with a railgun(supposedly dealing more damage?) gets you killed easilly, as the shotgun's fire rate is soo much higher.
I feel it's heavilly OP, and while i understand the lvl 1 weapon being so expensive should stay OP, the lvl 20 weapon shouldn't be soo much more powerful, allawing the other weapons if fully upgraded to catch up in damage and usability.  My point here not being "i dont' like the shotgun" but that it's becoming boring fighting only shotguns all over the place.
