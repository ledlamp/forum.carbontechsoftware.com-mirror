I would like this game mode personally, since unlike capture the flag you don't go back to your base. So the team that is suffering heavy losses would be pushed back to their spawn point and forced to defend, which is unlike capture the flag in which when the enemy takes your flag you actually go on the offensive and try to kill the flag carrier, while the enemy falls back with the flag.

There is however two changes I would like to be made to make the game mode more intense and more fun:

Each team gets a turret to defend the generator. Why? So that a player can't just abandon his base under attack to attack the enemy's undefended base. The turret can be destroyed, but it is rather deadly when there is only one target so you would need to try and destroy it while you are attacking the enemy's base in a group. Once the turret is destroyed the base is much more vulnerable and it would take a big comeback to take victory, but the flock of enemies right at your spawn would hopefully provide enough cash potential for players to stay. Which reminds me: when the turret gets a kill the last player who damaged the killed player is credited with the kill so no one gets their kill stolen by the map.

In the center of large maps, there is a KOTH style ring that when under control gives a new spawn point for the controlling team as well as a indestructible shield for their generator. Why? Because in giant maps you could easily reach the opponent's base, missing the other team on-route. If that were to happen it would create an awkward scenario where both teams are at the other team's base and the best course of action would be to try and destroy the generator right in front of you than travel all the way across the map and defend yours. I am thinking about you, Battle Island.
