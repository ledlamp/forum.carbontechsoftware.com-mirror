The Idea Project
posted Mid-October, 2012
I realize there isn't that many of us left, but I figured we are only going to lose members while CT hibernates so I figured I post this.


I want to try and bring back all the big ideas that were discussed over the period between the last update and now and put them together, sort them out and put them into one big document that we can have on hand just in case CT comes back. Every idea, no matter how popular (though it would likely be sorted based on popularity). That way a potentially great idea for the game is not lost due to this lull of absence of updates.


I think I could be able to create the final document, but I'd need help digging out as many ideas as possible. So if anyone wants to help, just post some forgotten ideas you have found in the forums or just remember from back ago as well as as much info about them as possible (suggested by, positive or negative response  how much talk was generated, anything you think is important).


It's a last-ditch effort to try and salvage some of the forum, at least for future versions we might not end up playing ourselves but to at least give Carbon Combat a potential future, and maybe some hope. Anyone knows of a previous idea that could have gotten implemented if there were just more frequent updates, please share it.


That is all.
