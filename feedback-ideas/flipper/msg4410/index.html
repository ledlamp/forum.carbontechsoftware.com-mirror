Inspired by robot wars, the flipper bodykit, if they are on it they would get flipped up into the air and forwards as well, spinning around, this would make them kind of vulnerable for a sec or 2 as well.


If you don't know what they are, basically they are low scoops at the front of a car that use hydraulics  mnemonics, or gas, to propel the scoop upwards very quickly, the gas ones are more powerful but the gas runs out after a while and the last shots are a bit weaker.


EDIT: RE-EXPLANATION


1. there is a low attachment to the front of the car which is a slightly slanted triangle, like a scoop,[this has given me a SEPERATE idea just now, maybe future cars could have hood and/or roof scoops]
2. in action, what it does is the mnemonics/hydraulics/gas moves the attachment upwards very quickly
3. This launches the car you have 'scooped' upwards
4. It takes a sec to return to its position
