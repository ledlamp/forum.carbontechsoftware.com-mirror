3 Kills: Shield powerup, as if you used a Temporary Shield item.
3 Kills: 10 seconds Temporary Invincibility
4 Kills: Instant health powerup
4 Kills: Random weapon drop, Lvl 20


Major Kill:

5 Kills: Repair bots

7 Kills: Projectiles slowly curve towards enemies for 30 seconds
7 Kills: Double damage for 30 seconds

3 Deaths: Half reload time for weapons that don't have one shot per clip

Major Loss:

5 Deaths: One attack bot
