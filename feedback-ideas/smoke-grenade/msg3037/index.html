Smoke Grenade
posted Mid-November, 2012
Well the title says everything. A rather exaggerated smoke grenade covering a wide area with smoke for getting past dangerous enemies without a fight to reach that flag/powerup. It would do minor damage with a direct hit on a car, and possibly frag damage. For the smoke, it would have to be around as big as the bases on Battle Island to be useful and it would have to fill up in less than 3 seconds and last at least 30 seconds.


Alternatively, they could be "ninja" smoke bombs that clog the windshield of those hit and blind the view of players with powder. There would be some visibility, but viewing distance is severely limited.


Both these would be in the form of grenades and so could be launched with more power by holding down the button.

Good point. Therefore I edit the proposition: anyone in the smoke is invisible on the minimap and does not have an arrow above their heads. That means ANYONE, including the person who fired the grenade. So it would create a space where people are cut off from their surrounding opponents, unless they literally bump into each other. You could switch out and fire weapons but your opponents could too.


And for the ninja smoke, that kinda is the point, to switch out and then kill them. It would make you more effective versus single opponents but it wouldn't help versus multiple enemies, since the blinding effect would wear off before you blind all your enemies and switch (it would be something around 15 seconds, maybe less).
