At first I thought you were describing a new car, but then I figured it out.


It is simply a bigger, slower version of the missile launcher. It fires bigger, slower missiles with a big punch, about that of the railgun. The catch: the missiles are slow moving and can be shot down. They aren't killable in one hit but they are pretty fragile. The turret which fires the missiles is also rather big, exposing a weakspot on the car. You can lock-on the missiles to a car by keeping the pointer on a car for 5 seconds.


A pretty simple idea, I kinda like it. We need to give it a corny name though, like "The Crusher" or "The Annihilator". All the giant missile launchers get names like that.


One problem: I could NEVER keep my cursor on a car for that long. I say it goes with the standard lock-on feature as seen with the standard missile launcher, or none at all.
