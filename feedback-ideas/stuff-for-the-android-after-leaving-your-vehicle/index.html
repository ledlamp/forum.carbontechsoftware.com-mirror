LONG POST WARNING!

Quote from: Name on Early September, 2012
Many players like and use the androids almost exclusively. Even though it was made as an afterthought, it is something to keep in mind for further updates. There is a lot of potential there.

Very good point, but there is one flaw. You see, androids are given full health when they get out of the car no matter the car's actual health. That makes the car the primary, "first" life of the player while the android is the "second", since androids can't re-enter cars. Given the fact that the android is already at the least annoying to cars even though they are statistically inferior to cars (the assault rifle is a Lvl. 1 minigun with half fire rate), that we already have an extensive car armory and that we don't want to encourage new players to think of the game as an FPS adding any more power to the android in any way is generally rejected by the community.

However, if we were to nerf the android a bit (remove the instant movement and create an acceleration based movement system, abet with a low top speed but an almost instant acceleration and turning rate is my suggestion, as said before in other topics) we would be able to start to add equipment options to the android, assuming they fit in balance with the game. If that were to happen I would more than be willing to encourage getting an android equipment store up and running.

  • Possibly, but needs to also make android less mobile to compensate, like the car armor. Lighter armor is pretty much not notable but heavy armor would leave a large dent.
