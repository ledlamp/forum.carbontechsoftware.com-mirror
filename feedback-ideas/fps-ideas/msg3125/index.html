Sniper rifles: Laser Sniper (Laser Cannon) and a regular Sniper (Railgun).

Obviously these would all be handheld versions and would generally have less power. What I suggest is taking the base weapon and reducing stats. I listed changes below compared to a level 1 car weapon:

Rocket Launcher: Two shot clip (Note that weapons would not have infinite ammo, though a reload animation would be nice it wouldn't be nessesary)
Plasma Rifle: Half the fire rate, bullet spread similar to Assault Rifle
Grenade Launcher: One shot clip, no charging grenades
EMP Grenade Launcher: One shot clip, no charging grenades
Homing Rocket Launcher: One shot clip
Flamethrower: Half damage, clip of 50
Assault Rifle: 20 shot clip with reload time of minigun, small increase in damage from current Assault Rifle (about 20%)
Sniper: Quarter damage, half reload time
Laser Sniper: 3/4s damage
Hook Launcher: Slightly shorter rope, increasing the chance of being rammed by the car the rope is attached to.
Shotgun: Half clip, half amount of pellets (lowers potential damage and the chance of a long range shot hitting), 3/4s damage

Anyone see a problem with these weapons?


Some of these weapons have been edited due to them not being balanced with each other. If a weapon seems overpowered (like the Laser Sniper), look at it a bit more closely. For instance with the Laser Sniper with 3/4s damage it can only take a car out in two, maybe even three hits, but there's the painfully long reload between shots that gives the car lots of time to fight back. Same for underpowered weapons: the shotgun for instance is a very good android killer.

Some things I would like to add:


Since you can carry two weapons on the car, in order for this system to work you would need to be able to have two weapons as an android.
