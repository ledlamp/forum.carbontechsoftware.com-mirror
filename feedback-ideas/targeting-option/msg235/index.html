Targeting option
posted Mid-July, 2012
Hello peoples.


Let's say I'm in a battle island Deathmatch with GTR34 and 6 others. Maybe I want to kill GTR because he gets annoying with across-the-map snipes. I would like it if there was a targeting option where I could single him out from the other 6 people so I can focus my fire on him instead of hitting some random dude who I really don't care much about.


It would simple highlight the target's vehicle locator arrow as a different color, like green. Note: Only the arrow would look different. Nothing else would change. Only one person would be targeted at a time.


It could also be used for defensive purposes instead of offensive. Let's say I want to massacre some not-so-hard-to-hit newbs on Sanctum and techietotoro is stalking me. I could use the targeting option to locate him so I can avoid him.


A possible way to turn on the targeting thing would be in the battle summary, next to the friend button, a crosshairs icon would appear. By clicking it, I could now see GTR34 and finish his remaining 30% health with my railgun, or I could run away from totoro.
If it doesn't work, whack it. If whacking it doesn't work, whack it again.
