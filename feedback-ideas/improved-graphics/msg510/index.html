The issue isn't with unity, nor with the lag, as most of us thought. GTR was right to keep this out of the equation.

The issue is with a "datafile" which acts as a loader of the environment of the game, and of the game functions.
i hope i wont' say stupid things(CT can correct me if will), but from what i understood in this file are stored also all the graphic details and physics. From this file the calls to photon are made, in client mode.

Now the issue is kongregate and many other gaming networks allaw datafiles of only 21MB(if i remember correctly what CT said), and tweaking the graphics would most likely make CC excede the datafile size, and thus not be publisheable on kongregate,thus loosing a potential wealth in audience of players.

Ofc if CC were to go solo, not being attached to any gaming network, or if the gaming networks were to rise the max dimension of the datafiles, then it woudl be a whole other story, but i doubt any of the 2 occurrences is in their immediate plans...
