It would be awesome in survival mode. My idea to make it useful:
  • Give insane protection, around 90%
  • Make it really fragile and easy to degrade
It would be a hit n' run armor like the ninja regen, but operates differently. It wouldn't be as good as say heavy armor in long-term engagements but it would be able to compete with certain weapons. That 90% is subtracted from the first hit and although damage degrades the armor instead of the # of hits, the fact that the first hit is degraded means snipers and other slow-firing weapons would be less effective. It would also mean that with two shotguns, the samurai armor wins if both players hit, so to counter you would need to be a bit tactical by whittling down the armor protection first, then going for the health hit quickly. Plus it would be just fun to use, in my opinion.
