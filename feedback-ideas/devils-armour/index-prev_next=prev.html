In-game PM system.
posted Late September, 2012
Many people in the game aren't on the forum. And what if you want to send them a message but they aren't online?


How bout have a PM system where you send a small message to anyone? Then if they are offline, you send them a message and they read it from their inbox when they log in!
New Ideas!  (Nov 28, 12)


You can access it from anywhere, including when you play and in the menu. It will tell you when you have a new message.


This will be great if you want to tell someone:
  • you can't join because the game is full
  • how to enter the game (rare, but I needed it once to tell players that they had to wait to have the highway race track load)
  • that you bought something
  • to chitter chatter
If you need to tell something to someone, but the recipient is not online, the PM system will have your message in the recipient's inbox and the recipient can read it the next time he/she/it goes online. Also, most players aren't on the forum or visit rarely. The in-game PM system allows you to send messages to any registered player.


Features it should have:
  • Block users
  • Delete buttons
  • Archive buttons

New Idea!  (Dec 3, 12)

Idea: Access your message center (inbox and compose) from the forum!
What if you can't load CC and your friend is expecting you? or what if your game crashed and then you g2g (got to go)? You can send them a quick message from the forum without having to load the game.
