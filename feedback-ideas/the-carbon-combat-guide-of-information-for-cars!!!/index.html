UPDATE 1.1: Flying Speeds
  • These results were formed in custom maps
  • /flylock was on for forward tests of flying
               -/flylock is on to provide a control group for the given information
  • other tests were tested flying straight up
  • No armor added

First Car: Combat Car
Flying straight up, the car's top speed was 78 mph. Flying forward with /flylock on, the car had a top speed of 88 mph; an increase of 10 mph.


 
Second Car: The Sprinter
Flying straight up, this car had a top speed of 96 mph. Flying forward with /flylock on, the car had a top speed of 104 mph; an increase of 8 mph.


 
Third Car: The Racer
Flying straight up, this car had a top speed of 78 mph. Flying forward with /flylock, this car had a top speed of 89 mph; an increase of 11 mph.


 
Final Car: The Devastator
Flying straight up, this car had a top speed of 72 mph. Flying forward with /flylock, this car had a top speed of 83 mph; an increase of 11 mph.
