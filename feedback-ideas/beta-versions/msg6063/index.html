Beta versions
posted Early June, 2013
Once the game has been balanced out instead of immediately releasing updates that are not bug fixes, there should be a beta version open to only some people to avoid something like the laser being one shot and the shotgun when it first came out happening. The beta testers can decide whether a weapon is too OP or UP by playing with it in the beta and then they can test a new version of it. I feel this is needed to give future versions a more professional feel rather than just doing an update as quickly as possible without testing it. The beta should be compatible with accounts so that you can have all your equipment, but any new additions to the shop that you could buy can be rented free, for any testers who don't have much creds.

The testers wouldn't be all pros,  there would be a mixture of the level of skill and equipment. We would have to find people for this though as most of us in the forum are pros.

Edit: I know that it says beta besides the version number, but really it is open to everyone and isn't really a beta. Also I remembered that the beta is more the last test before something is released, you guys know what I mean anyway, more of an initial testing.
