It could be an item in the form of a radar dish on your car, but I personally like the idea of it being a weapon actually. If you were to use the radar only when it was equiped then it would be pretty useful but not overpowered since the weapon switch time would force you to use the radar in a safe location, usually away from enemies making it give only a general location of them (on the minimap a player off the radar would appear as a dot on the edge of the minimap, and the names wouldn't be precise enough for actual usage) and it would take up a weapon slot, a rather big disadvantage. It would also be a great weapon/item for team support since as long as you can communicate clearly you really only need one per team.

If you still don't like the idea, I think there could be some less powerful item since the switch times are no longer in place as a counterbalance, such as (off the top of my head) flares that when activated pop a flare/firework from every enemy car, revealing invisible opponents but only for a few seconds.
