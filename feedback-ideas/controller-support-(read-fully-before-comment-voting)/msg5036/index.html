Hello all, this thread is the first of two I will post today, one now, one later.
We all know that video game controllers are common in our daily lives, and that we use them for all most all of our consoles. But why must we just use the controllers we already have on consoles? Well, we don't, because some of us have our controllers hooked up to our computers and play games with them. So why can't we use them for Carbon Combat? Well, CarbonTech hasn't put in this feature (yet), which is why I made this thread. There are three main controllers you can use for PCs:
The Xbox 360 controller:

Logitech (F series mostly):

And the more un-common PS3 DS3 controller:

(The Xbox and F710 can be/are wireless)
Now before anyone complains, the you would be able to customize the controls as you wish, but there will be default ones too. Also, the PS3 Dual-Shock 3 and Xbox 360 controllers support chat-pads:
PS3 DS3 Chat Pad:

Xbox 360 Chat-Pad:

Now your hands don't have to leave the controller
So, as a summary what I'm proposing is:
Controller Support for most main brands (Logitech, Microsoft, etc...)
Chat-Pad Support for the Controllers that use them (Xbox 360 and DS3)
Customizable controls (With optional default)
Comments and Constructive Criticism is welcomed.
