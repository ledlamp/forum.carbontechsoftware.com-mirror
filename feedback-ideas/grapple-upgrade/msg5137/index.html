Grapple Upgrade
posted Late February, 2013
Well we all know grapple is just for fun... literally, you can't really use it for crap in battle. the only way you can kill is if collision damage is on and you drag  the person to death, granted the don't hover or YOU dont kill yourself first. and the only other use would be to grapple the player who's the most threat to you team in TDM and CTF. even then, they still might shoot you.
soooo, i think the grapple should have another fun aspect. it's very simple actually: the grapple usually pulls a person right? but what if you didnt HAVE to drive to pull the person? there would be a new button ( any one we don't use that's close to WASD, doesnt matter) that would reel the person you grappled toward you (it would be press and hold). you don't even have to move! i think you should also be able to press another button (sorry for all the button, I don't think a toggle would work for this since it's pulling/pushing you) and it would let the line go slack as the distance between you increase (basically it'll let the person do whatever but once you let go the line it locks again)
Also, tired of having your friend have to exit the car so they can slowly pull you? well there would be another button (most likely F since it's a nice toggle button) that would reverse the direction and let the person you grappled pull you!
both of these combined will make the non-damaging grapple an epic weapon.. or should i say tool! tired of falling down that stupid mountain? are you always getting stuck when bungee jumping? well you need the handy dandy grappling hooook! XD lol, that way you can reverse the direction, press and hold reel, and pull yourself up the 90 degree cliff! ( all spy/batman like XD). comments? questions?
