Let's break it down one by one, because half of it is just nonsense.


anyway, dude, how can  a vehicle have a certain amount of health?! the health depends on whether or not you have armour


All right, I'm going to stop you there. Let me remind you something: OUR CARS HAVE HEALTH, REMEMBER? That was what he was referring to. The health bar would be 3/4s that of the car, or 25% more vulnerable to damage. If you are still confused, then think of it like this: the android has 1/2 of the health of a car.


why does liftoff have to be so different? why cant we make it like the car, theres no difference between they're wheels


It's simple: the bike would be a more physics based vehicle. Things like instead of turning left you lean left, turning the wheels to the left instead of just turning the wheels left would create a more accurate bike riding experience. The same would be for the air: instead of the body of the car moving, the wheels would move pushing the body and possibly rotating and flipping it if you can't control it. For the record though, this was a good question.


if it will have top speed AND accel, maybe even handling? why dont we make it that it cant fly? with all the other attributes it can launch up mountains 80 degrees steep or less going at least 50 mph


As said by answering you previous question, it would NOT have maximum handling. It would be a smaller and faster target but it wouldn't be say android level on avoiding gunfire; it still is propelled by wheels and wheels need to turn for a bit before it gets to top speed, plus the vise-versa of a complete stop taking longer. When flying it would move faster but still be an easy target for snipers and missile launchers, even more so that evasive maneuvers such as flips are much harder to pull off.


maybe you can even move in air like a dirtbike! you can press WASD to rotate at flip. while pressing space you can also barrel roll


If you can give me one practical use for this I would fully agree, but it just wouldn't have a use. Plus the space bar scopes, remember?


i dont see how thats possible, all the weapons have the same scope, and the cars can aim forward too.


I'm going to take a shot in the dark and say you are referring to the turret being on the front of the bike. What that means is that you can aim forward DOWN as well as straight forward. The car would need to be lower than the target in order to shoot at them, at least forwards. The bike would not have this problem, though with 3/4s health it would less suited for sitting still and firing away due to return fire taking out the bike first. It would have to be limited to a run & gun style.
