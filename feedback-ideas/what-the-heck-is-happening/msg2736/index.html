What the heck is happening?
posted Late October, 2012
Hello ladies and gents,

TheGoodGuy here, with a question that I think is as absurd to ask as it's need to be asked - "What the heck is going on with the game?"

Has this game just stopped being developed? Has everyone left the game? Is there anyone else playing this game other than A.I. bots and non-chatting guests? Why has this happened?

There are a lot of subsidiary questions, but the root question is - "When, if ever, should we expect more activity on this game?"

I love the game, but if there is no one playing it, or if there is nothing new that peaks my interest - then why would anyone else play the game? Do you see the issue now?

I would imagine if there was more development towards the enhancements, more people would play the game. As it is, it appears many of the suggestions for improvements (mine included) have fallen by the wayside.

Now, I'm sure there is a reason for this. I feel the more prudent course of action if nothing can be done for some time is to AT LEAST keep everyone apprised of WHEN something might come about because otherwise you risk losing long-term players due to the lack of communication.

Thoughts everyone?

Cheers,
TheGoodGuy
