This is all very beautiful, but if you ever hope CT to implement using your ideas, your diagram is going to need a few things;
  • Frame of reference - He needs to know how big / small the scale is. Looking at the car as the frame of reference, I think it would be too small to be useful outside of grenade-and-shotgun-spamming.
  • Quantifying - Are the boundaries of the minimum / medium / maximum going to be analogue or digital? If digital, what timescales do you suggest? If analogue, what are the ranges?
  • Explaining - What will happen to bullets, what will happen to rockets, grenades? If you have any knowledge of programming, will it affect all the arrays that the car produces while in the timescale? If so, how will this be achieved? How much will it cost? How long will the effects last?
Its a nice idea, but you should really focus on developing it so it's as easy as possible for CT to implement it; remember, if a job is worth doing well, it's worth doing yourself.
