New Death Animation/Graphic
posted Late April, 2013
Currently, when our health had reached zero, we BLOW UP *KA-BOOM*

And I see it happen every time I shoot somebody out of the air. Shoot 'em with a Grenade out of the air, and they are gone. But we see the same explosion EVERY SINGLE TIME. My idea is to simply make a graphic or new way for our cars to die. I have no problem if you die while you are on the ground, our animation for the current explosion should stay.

BUT, what about if your shot down out of the air? What SHOULD happen is our engines die, we spin out of control in the last 1-2 directional keys we used and fall until we hit the ground. Then blow up.

Example, You have 10% health, get shot with a missile, and you fall to your car loses stability, NO WAY OUT OF IT, until you hit the ground. You cannot even get out of the car while you are falling. That would be just W-A-C-K.

In conclusion, we are simply adding a new way of dying. no extra, life or extra damage. You just die differently.

Whatcha think, fellas?
