This is...interesting. I like the idea of being able to choose your killstreak, and it would be interesting to see how it plays out. I'm really liking it for several reasons:
  • Feels like you can get your own "personal" favorite bot, if they are balanced correctly. More customization without any increase in power, yet you are more effective due to your killstreak fitting your playstyle. For instance I would love those repair bots myself being a heavy armor user and not a ninja regener.
  • Rewards players with more skill. This is because say I like repair bots, but if I'm not smart I call them while I have full health, or if I level up my bots and am unable to cash in on the double killstreak (note on double killstreaks below).
  • Counterable. If an enemy player gets a shield bot, you can either be an idiot and try to shoot the car or you can blow the shield first and shoot the bots. Once the bots are dead, the players are on even ground, but it takes a smart and skilled player to know to shoot the small bots.
  • Anti-bot weapons becomes better. The shotgun is okay against bots but they can be just too mobile to deal with the shotgun. You want a weapon that can shoot them at a distance and reloads fast enough to deal with multiple bots, with damage not being as much a factor. That means plasma blaster, minigun, and maybe missile launcher if the player is good with it, weapons that right now don't see a lot of use with the decline of sniper rifles.
I think there should be just one change, however. The killstreak and double killstreak requirements need to come down. 6 is already a pretty large amount of kills for one life, and you want to try for 12? That's only going to happen in super easy rooms, and would encourage farming or just not be used at all for the majority of the time.


Otherwise, cool idea. I never saw this before, I think. I'll check out the original thread now.
