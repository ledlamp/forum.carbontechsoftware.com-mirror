I personally wouldn't be interested. If you take one look at the IOS/Android market you see our competition is mostly social MMO style games. The gaming audience we are sharing wouldn't be interested in a action shooter, and the community we would gain isn't exactly the best.

That said, there is a LOT of money in the mobile market. If we slap a .99 cent price tag on the game, a total failure game would give about 40 buys. The current version of CC could go for a higher price, and be able to get more buys. However the networking would be completely horrible, hence the lack of action shooters in the market. The best solution I can come up with is to convert the mobile game to a single player game with bots, and maybe use a previous version.
