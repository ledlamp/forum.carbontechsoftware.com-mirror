New Map and Concept
posted Late February, 2013


Hello Carbon Combat Community!

As you allay know, Carbon Combat has some pretty amazing maps. From the crashed ship on Battle Island to the open hatches on Silos. Each map is unique and has a certain feel to them.

But I want to bring in a new "feel" for a new map. And at first it may seem a little hard and/or "unlinked," but please let me explain.

The new map idea is a city. Not just any city, but a city made specifically for Carbon Combat.

Now you may be saying something like, "That is just not fitting the CC Theme we have."

Now I say this:
Carbon Combat needs a new feeling. A feeling of modern society; much like the cities and freeways we have today. of us, including myself, do not live in cities with majorly high skyscrapers and huge 40 story tall business buildings. Some of us live in the suburbs. So I incorporate my idea with people's surroundings to create a map that is both rich in smaller houses and bigger industrial buildings.

Please comment on what you think. Even I am a bit "iffy" about the idea, but please let me know first what you believe.
