Better Game Physics Idea
posted Mid-April, 2013
Many of us know that our flying cars are pretty awesome. We defy gravity in our own little way.
 
But has anyone ever stopped to ask, "How exactly do we get off the ground?" Well, we Just fold out our wheels and take off; simple as that.
 
However, I stopped and thought to myself, "Doesn't someone know how much friction is put under four wheels why traveling 90 mph? A lot. When the wheels fold out, from the ground, they look like this:

Now, if I am correct, 90 mph is 130 ft per second. That speed is enough to travel a football field in 2 seconds. Apply a direct amount of friction to that speed and you have a little problem.
 
Now, our game graphics are just fine, as many know. I just wish to make a the game's physics look better. Though it is not needed, it will improve game graphics. I am all for improved game graphics in Carbon Combat.
 
Any thoughts on how we can make this "rubbing of wheels on the ground" look go away?
