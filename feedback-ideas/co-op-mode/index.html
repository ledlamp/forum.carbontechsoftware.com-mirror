co-op mode
posted Late September, 2012
This would be a big addition but a fun one: special co-op versions of the normal cc car, sprinter and racer.


They're car is bigger and there is one turret for the gunner, works like normal turret, 2 weapon options. Also there is a hatch that protects the gunner that could incorporate the existing armours, but weaker.
The driver would have a smaller turret, maybe like a machine gun (see the machine gun thread) that is not changeable and is on the bonnet of the car.


The drivers view would be like the existing one but the gunners view would be like the scope view, but you can zoom in and out.


There would be a 'switch option' where if one player presses it it shows on the other players screen and if they press it too they switch (in maybe 5 seconds), if the driver bails it will also tell the gunner and they would be able too switch as soon as they press the button without their team mate pressing it.
Also if there is one person still in the car it would not explode and within a certain radius around the car and maximum speed of maybe 10 mph the person out side the car could get by holding g for 3 seconds, it could be possible in a team game for two people in different cars without someone else in their car to join forces into one car (you exit your car and enter the others whilst they're under 10 mph, the other would explode)


In a co-op death match if the gunner dies they would respawn as a person and the driver could pick him/her up, if the driver dies then he/she would respawn in the car, if both are inactive (waiting to respawn) (died at the same time) then the time is added together and they respawn together.




Please give me a suggestion on how to lay it out better if you find it difficult too read.
