The bike in my opinion actually seems rather balanced on paper. The thing most people are forgetting about the android's speed is not it's actual speed, but it's acceleration, being able to change directions almost instantly to dodge bullets (unlike the car). The bike would not have that advantage. The weapon ideas are nice like missile pods and mounted chainguns but most would have the disadvantage of being only front firing, so a bike would have to use it's superior agility to line up with it's target and the car would have to use it's 360 weapon systems to shoot down a hoverbike before it can line up and deal damage. There would also be a rather clear and large weak spot on the bike (the rider) and it would be rather vulnerable to regular androids.

Even if it is overpowered, it can always be an alternative to the car with the ability to eject (though the ejecting android is bound to have taken damage).
