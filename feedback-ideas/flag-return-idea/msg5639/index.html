Hello CCC! (In case you are wondering: Carbon Combat Community)

I was in a match of CTC (Capture the Flag) when I saw that a n00b grabbed the flag and went outside the bounds of Chokepoint. Great, what a move.

Anyhow, I do not remember if it had been suggested before now, but how about a timer on the flag. Maybe, 30 seconds?

By timer, I mean how long a flag can be on it own without being back at one of the bases or with a player. Once it's dropped a timer appears above it to show how much time it has before appearing back at the base. Once a player picks up the flag, the timer goes away.

If the player was to drop it again, the timer would restart at: 30 seconds.
