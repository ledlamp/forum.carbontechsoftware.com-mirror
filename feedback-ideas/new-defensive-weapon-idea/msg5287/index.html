New defensive weapon idea
posted Early April, 2013
Hello everyone!

From the great minds of U2M and [mod]Nattyfrank comes a brand new weapon idea!

This new weapon will be for defensive uses and to escape from an attacker.  When you shot this weapon it will shot a high speed net that, upon contacting the target, will expand to form a giant sphere around the target.  The target will not be able to leave the sphere until it crumbles to dust. 

While the target is entombed by the sphere, they cannot be harmed. 

Why would anyone use this weapon that doesn't do damage?  Well, this weapon can be used while holding a flag during a ctf match, only a few times while holding the flag. 

Some proposed names are:
  • Reinforcer
  • Quantum (Ray, Gun, etc)
  • The Terminal
  • The Parameter
  • Optical Fiber Ray
Upgrading this weapon will increase the size of the sphere, as well as the duration that it lasts.  At level 20, the net will be able to capture two combat cars sitting side by side, while at level one, it will capture only one combat car.

Also, it can be shot at yourself! 

Currently I am not sure of the price or any of the stats, that will come at a later time.

But for now, discuss below and U2M and I will do our best to answer all questions!
Respectfully,
[Mod] Nattyfrank
