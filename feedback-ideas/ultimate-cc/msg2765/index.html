Ultimate cc
posted Late October, 2012
This idea would allow the game to develop itself as well as adding a whole load of extra features which would bring in new players, the only thing is though, it would take a lot of time so this is probably going to be like all the other posts on the forum.


Offline Career

There would be a world which is based on a metal/carbon mix, there are 5 factions, 2 good and 3 bad: The bureaucrats, The psychos, The mercenaries (controlled by 'the employer'), the revolutionists and the heroes.

whichever team you join gives you 5 alternate endings, one for each faction victory (complete control over the planet) each faction has it's advantages and disadvantages

The bureaucrats
This faction would have a lot of money and a police force converted to militants the downside would be that it has a small force and the troops do not have advanced equipment.

The psychos
this faction would have access to heavy twisted weaponry, a medium force and the downside is that their craziness would cause some weird involuntary actions,like occasional random firing, random driving and such...

The mercenaries
This group would have tough armour and access to heavy weapons (not as good as the psychos), the downside is when ever the leader is killed all the other players on the team leave, beacause they are paid by that person)

The revolutionists
these people would have a large amount of troops but not much money and equipment

The heroes
These people would be a small group of elite people with elite weapons

the good people can co op with the other good and vice versa for the bad people, but then have opportunities to double cross each other (the heroes want a government and the rev's want communism)

Online career

the same as normal career except of course everyones real people, you join one team and stick with it until one team wins then the war starts again and you can pick another side (the war would last days and you log in sometimes to help, so there would be high and low action points, only high ranked people can go on the heroes team.

Self developing

There can be a turret editor, armour editor, and mines and items and bodykits editor, with sliders and a max limit (slightly higher limit for premium items) adjusting things like aoe, damage, protection, durablility, times you can use the item, ammo, reload and so on...
this is optional but it would be good to add a garage to this.

Also a game mode editor.

mods can overview the things and decide the ones useable to use and buy in-game, all things can be used in 1 player arcade games (explained later) against bots, and al things can be viewed in a new menu tab called 'showcase'

milestones, achievments and team wins online earns you points, points buys you the ability to buy custom things in the single player career and rent perks.

Multiplayer arcade

This would be another tab, this is much like the existing cc but you can also play games against killer bots and ai bolts like 'raribel' without money and xp and can use op unallowed weapons (unallowed elsewhere)


All things bought or unlocked are only bought or unlocked in the certain game (career, online career) unless otherwise stated

How things work in the online wars

when you attack you arrive in a spawn ship, if it is destroyed you can't respawn unless some one repairs it (can be a item like the fusion cutter in star wars battlefront)
defending team gets auto turrets at their base but they arrive at their base only after the alarm has gone (detected enemies) + organisation time

maps that don't fit in with the story line can be changed or scrapped in career e.g instead of flag in ctf, an info item or something that a ship is arriving to collect.

The ranking system gives you titles and more unlocked items and resources from your team, and control over your inferiors.

given the work if this, there can be a paid game and a limited game, the limited would be, can use arcade multiplayer like normal, but in both careers, no ranking up, unlocking or anything, and you stay a 'grunt' with guest features

I will add other things that I've forgotten or haven't thought of yet.
