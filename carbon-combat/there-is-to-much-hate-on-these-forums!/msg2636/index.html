o23456, this community is falling apart because it is not used to stagnant game development. The community is used to updates and the excitement of a growing community. Disagreements/fights that occurred before were considered unimportant or miniscule because there was always development, and it was regarded as more significant. Therefore, people were more content/tolerant. Now, the creator, CarbonTech, has been absent for over three months now, and the favorable community has consequently ceased to exist. "Fights" are relentless; many continue them because they don't know something better to do in this community.


Take the disagreements between Lamp and Cremyjr, for example. Their disagreements/fights all of a sudden escalated quickly over the past three months, whereas it wasn't as much of a concern before. Also, XD used to love this game so much, but when development stopped, it all of a sudden became the worst game he ever played.

My point is, I urge everyone to wait with patience and tolerance. This community will survive if we let it. The drama and personal retaliations must lessen if this community wishes to thrive. However, if one truly wishes to leave, he should leave in silence, or maybe detach himself from the game while staying loosely connected.


With warm regards,


Mod Eric
