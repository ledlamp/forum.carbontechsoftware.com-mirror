All right, I see what is happening here. For the record, this should be in the FAQ, it really is frequently asked.

To play with your Kongregate Log-in, create a new account on the Carbon Combat play page with the exact same name (punctuation and grammar counts) as your Kongregate account, but a different password. Once that account is created they should automatically synch, giving you the ability to purchase Premium Coins. They are premium and so cost REAL money but there are some third-party offers you can complete for free Premium Coins, but there is no fault on Carbon Combat if these offers do anything wrong (IE give away personal info) so be careful which ones you choose to complete.
