Hey everybody,

As you probably know, I'm mostly (only) known around here for being a Whirld designer on Carbon Combat and Mars Explorer. Now that we have a new forum, none of my amazing custom worlds are posted here!
Since a lot of people seem to play them (more in CC than on Mars), I thought I should just post all of them here, on this one thread, for easy access.

So here they are, all 21 of my custom worlds! These are not the regular type of custom worlds, designed in the world creator - they are made in Unity3D, the program with which Carbon Combat is develloped.
