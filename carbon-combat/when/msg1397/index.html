I think I may have just found a temporary solution to some of our problems.
 
How to (somewhat) remove a user from the forum

  • Step 1: Go to your profile page, section Look and Layout (you will need to go to another profile page first to see the link). Check "Hide post from users on my Ignore list". Remember to save changes.
  • Step 2: Go to profile page section Personal Messaging. Change your "Receive personal messages from:" bar to anything other than All Members.
  • Step 3: Go to profile page, section Buddies/Ignore List. Go to the ignore list and type in the name of any member/members you wish to get rid of.
There you go. If any users are particularly annoying or just derailing your conversation, use this to completely ignore them. You may or may not wish to tell other users that you might be a step behind, but usually if there is something important it will be in a quote from someone not on your ignore list.
