It's called hosting your own game and telling ppl to join.

Bots never join games. They only host. And they load the map the same time you join. When you join a bot server, they all spawn at the same time you do. This is why you see some bot servers running "KingDvo's King Island". Dynamics are also very accurate so I believe that they are run off your computer, and when more than one ppl join, it goes networked.  I think they are hosted on the first person to join the server because they leave when the first person who joined does; and they leave at the same time.
In the newest version of Carbon Combat, you can only have 1, 2, or 4 players in Battle Island (which is odd because Battle Island is a big map). The servers with more than 2 or 4 player limit was originally a bot server.


Bots also use an older version of Carbon Combat. I know because when there is a guest bot, it has a number (e.g. guest_23456).


Bots I believe was added by CT because originally the game was very quiet and had usually only 1-5 servers running— usually 1 or 2 and RARELY 5 or more. (I am not sure about this but I saw in pics and vids that there are very little servers running.)
