Edit: Poll added. Also, this picture is at least 2 weeks ago. Apparently this happens more often than I thought.

I've been meaning to post this topic for so long now, so the picture is a bit dated. That shouldn't matter, though.

So basically, I am sick of TinyChat. Normally I don't use it too much, but whenever I do it's always chaotic (see the attachment). The problems are pretty obvious:
  • No log-in: Anyone can come and just start spamming/flamming with no way to identify and stop them. Plus impersonation has become a problem.
  • Bad moderation: The one (or occasionally none) moderator the chat assigns could be at the very best completely incompetent. At worst, it is a spammer/flammer who uses the privileges against normal users, and even forum moderators.
  • Front page: If this were a good chat then this wouldn't be a problem, but with the state it is right now it just degrades the forum face appeal. Imagine someone following the link to see if the forum is something interesting to follow, and they see the big white box on the bottom right.
Basically, it needs to go. Replaced, hopefully, maybe with less features, but right now it's a mess.
