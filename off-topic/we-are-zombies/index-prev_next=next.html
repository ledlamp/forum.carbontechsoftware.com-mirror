Awesome Ninja Stars
posted Early October, 2012
I made these myself.
The third one flies really well but the only reason it has tape is to make sure it doesn't fall apart.
The first and second are Kalami stars. They're just for looks and don't fly. Look pretty cool but they're hard to make and take practice. The first was the first time I made it. It's flimsy since the construction paper was thick, and that made it hard to fold. The third was done with copy paper and is better in terms of how well it is made but doesn't look as nice.


Links:
  • 16 Pointed Ninja Star: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PRRMGdo41wk
  • Kalami Star: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GjqybPt9f4M
Feel free to post the ones you make if you try to make them.


Note: The second one looks crappy (looks like plastic to me) because of my scanner. I did my best to adjust the brightness and contrast.
