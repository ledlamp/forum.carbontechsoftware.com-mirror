Hey everyone, techietotoro here.

CT and myself have been very busy with school over the past few months. Neither of us has enough time to manage everything we try to do. In order to do the things we need to do in real life, we've had to cut CC from our daily lives.

I'm sorry that I didn't say anything earlier and that we haven't been around. CC has been quite the project for both of us, and I'm not keen on giving up on it so soon, even if it has been nearly two years in development! I'm really happy to see that this forum is thriving to some degree, and the game more so. For the first time, I played in a game where both scores were above 100!

Regarding that thing about potential CC dev team: I am that team. You may know that I've been with the game from the start, and that I created both Chokepoint and Sanctum. I love CC and I have been quite excited to see how it would turn out.


I'm going to talk to CT to see if he can release at least one more version of Carbon Combat. I don't know what his plans are, but hopefully a new release could squash some of the nastier bugs the game has been recently experiencing. When a new version comes out, it will likely be the final one. I'm not sure about that, but by judging the waters, I'm pretty sure that will be the case.

You guys have been a great community, audience, and support for myself and CT. For that, I'd like to give a big thank-you to each and every one of you, especially to those who have since left the forum.

And hopefully, if all goes well, a bigger and better *new* project awaits!
