Fan Fic Project
 
 
   This will be the host thread for my fan-fic project I am starting. Inspired by PanzerAce, re-inspired by U2M, I am going to use this post as both the guidelines, staffing and writing of the project, in that order.

Guidelines
 
  • This project is a COMMUNITY project, and we will be accepting writers, editors, and content writers. Writers and editors will be put on a contract (read agreement) for a certain section of writing or period of time, but content writers can submit their own pieces of content. Upon agreeing to the terms of your sign-on contract you are assumed to have your duties done by a certain date if required, so if you don't think you can focus your time on something that might take a good while, do not sign up. Once you have completed your contract you are released from the fan-fic project unless you wish to sign up again, so you can ask to to a guest-write or edit for the duration of one piece of writing. Content writers do not write on a contract and so can submit anything they think may help the project in the posts below or by PM. Content can include anything from pictures and documents to accompany the project to fancy banners to use. You will not be featured as a content writer unless the specific content is used.
  • Each section is an "episode", and they will be released on predetermined dates. These dates are determined the previous episode's release. They will all be featured on this post and will generally be about as long as a short chapter in a book (10-20 decent paragraphs), though they may be shorter on some occasions. The plan for the next episode is written along with each episode, so there is no long-term plan or even a determined end. All episodes will be dated for reference, so remember to look at each episode's date before you make a comment.
  • This is a collaboration, and anyone willing to collaborate in any way should send me a PM with details. This should include something like contract terms you would want, your own personal Carbon Combat related writing you would like to share/incorporate with this project, ect.
  • Our first goal is to get our feet in before we start dreaming too big, and so we will start with small episodes before we start doing full episodes. This will continue to at least episode 5, then we will try and increase the size of each episode.
Staffing

Staffing notices will be posted here throughout the project. Anyone currently on or has contributed to the project will also be listed here.
  • FatCatAttack, Replacement Writer & Editor, Content Writer. Contract: FOREVER MUHAHAHAHA
Currently we are looking for anyone willing to sign a contract for episode 2, 3, 4, or 5 (I will handle the first episode). These are short episodes and so if anyone is unsure if they want to write longer episodes then this would be a good place to see how well you do on an easier section. Content writers please reserve until episode 1 is out.
We are also looking for guest stars for anyone willing to star in the Fan-fic with their username. Note that your general personality will also be used (as perceived by the writer) and you may be killed off at any time.

Writing

The first episode is due Friday, September 21st at midnight. The episode plan is already worked out and those interested in joining the project can be able to see it. If you are antsy, then the below attachments will give you a hint.
