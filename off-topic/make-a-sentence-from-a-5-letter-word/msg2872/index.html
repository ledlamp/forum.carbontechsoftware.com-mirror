Make a sentence from a 5-letter word
posted Early November, 2012

I thought this forum was awfully quiet so I thought I would post a forum game.


Make a sentence from a 5 letter word in the order they are given.
First reply starts the game. Then the next reply should say the sentence and then give another word.

Examples:
Post 1 (starts the game):
ROAMS
Post 2:
Raymond offered a mouse seeds.


RAMPS
Post 3:
Ralph ate my precious sandwitch.

CAMPS
And so on...

Note: Bold text is not required.




                                                                First reply starts the game!
