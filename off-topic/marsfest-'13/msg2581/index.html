Hey guys! Remember me?
Well anyhow, here at mars, we have this thing called Marsfest. It is semiannual and this is its seconds marsfest. It is really fun and we were typing thing so funny that we literally cracked up laughing BEFORE WE EVEN TYPED ANYTHING! Hahahah!
So here is the post I made for the second semiannual marsfest. Enjoy...








ITS ALMOST HERE! The bi-annual marsfest is coming up, folks! The first-ever winter marsfest (yes, there is the summer marsfest and a winter marsfest) will be the biggest marsfest ever. The theme: SNOW. Yes, snow. Pretty realted, isn't it.
 
 
So here is what I want YOU to do! Read this post and FOLLOW everything I SAY.
 
What I need you, the community, to do.
I made a list of things that will take you no more than 5 minutes and a single PM:
 
  • PM me at one world that has a winter theme (Do not say GTR's world, folks. That's too easy.)
  • PM me the name of a world, that is not winter themed,  that you feel will be good for marsfest '13
  • PM me a RSVP if you want to join
Now the rest will take you just a short period of time of mars explorer playing:
  • Tell registered and un-registered people about the marsfest and if they will want to be in it.
  • Tell them to PM me if they want to join.
  • PM me all of the people's usernames that accepted your invitation
Rules of Marsfest
The rules of marsfest are the same as the previous:
 
  • There are 2 types of marsfest servers: dogfighting servers and party servers. Each server will clearly show what type it is.
  • You must RSVP to be in marsfest. If you aren't on the list, you will be evicted from the server.
  • All servers are password protected. Party Servers will always have the same password. This year, the password is: "snowy" Dfing servers will have various passwords. The host picks them.
  • Anything can happen in Party Servers.
  • In DFing servers, you cannot talk. A simple verbal shoutout is tolerable.
  • In DFIng servers, the host decides if he should let you in and if he should evict you.
  • If a server collapses, wait 200 seconds for the host to rehost the server. If he does not do so, join another marsfest server. If there is no marsfest server, host one. Then wait 300 seconds for people to join. If no one joins, then disconnect and join a marsfest server (because someone must've hosted when you did) And to the hosts: don't join another server, just recreate a server and wait for people to come. If no one comes within 300 seconds, you can disconnect your server and join a new one.
  • If you notice that a party server has reached its limit of 10 people, tell the host that. He will then pick a person to host another server. And so forth... The rule applies for DFing servers to, except that the limit is 7 instead of 10.
  • You cannot be evicted from Party Servers unless if you are not on the RSVP list. When I say "you" I mean the person who created your account. If there is suspicion that you are a puppet, you will be evicted immediatly.
  • Certain people have the right to enter Marsfest Servers even though they aren't on the RSVP list. You are qualified if you have made a comment or post on the forums that dated before July 31st, 2009.
  • Marsfest lasts for 8 days.
  • The Rules probably will change. I took out and added a lot of rules so check everyday.

 
Other Things
This post is an organization post. It is just a message that I am organizing Marsfest '13. When I am done, I will make a second post that consists of a world list, RSVP list, and more information about the marsfest. I hope you have fun and can make it!
